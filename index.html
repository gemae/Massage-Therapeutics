Boorman Equine Bodyworks

Strives to:

  • Help your horse be comfortable and able to perform to their maximum capacity
  • Meet each horse where they are, working within their comfort zone to identify and resolve restrictions
  • Partner with your vet to help maintain soft tissue health
  • Pre-emptively identify and address muscular issues as they emerge, providing a whole horse hands-on surveillance and records of changes to areas of muscular tension
  • Utilize a variety of customized non-invasive non-medical holistic modalities
