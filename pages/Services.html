Cranialsacral Therapy

Craniosacral therapy connects the equine therapist to the central nervous system of the horse. It leverages the subtle pulse created by the generation of cerebrospinal fluid in the cranium and transmitted down the spine to the sacrum. CST is a very gentle technique that works to realign the skeletal structure and relaxes the animal. The gentle work to remove restrictions to the free flow of fluid through the CNS can be immensely helpful in bringing balance to horses who carry emotional stress. I often integrate elements of this technique into my holistic bodywork sessions. A CST only session lasts about 30 minutes, but can vary significantly.

$40

Kinesiology Tape Session

This technique has long been used on human athletes and as its effectiveness continues to be demonstrated for horses, its use is becoming more common. It pairs well with a holistic massage or after a chiropractic treatment, to promote and support the effect of treatment. Ktape can activate or relax muscle activity depending on the application. It can be used to support and stabilize tendons and ligaments while allowing full range of motion.
